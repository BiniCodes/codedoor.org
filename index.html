SUPPORT REFUGEES AND THOSE IN NEED TO BECOME DEVELOPERS

CodeDoor is an organization that helps refugees to continue their education and to learn programming languages

 
Contribute
HOW IT WORKS


For Students and Teachers
Register for free, start to learn how to code and join other students and tutors on and through CodeDoor.org.


For Donors
Donate a used operational laptop to the refugees, so they can learn how to code.

Learn more about our mission
For Developers
Join the CodeDoor developer community to code open source modules for and with the refugees and if you want even act as a tutor.


"50 million people in the world today have been forcefully displaced from their home ... only 20% are in school." We have 21st century educational technology at our fingertips. It's time we start asking: How can we let refugees thrive in the internet era?”

The CodeDoor Team
CodeDoor Foundation Established 2015

Meet a student
